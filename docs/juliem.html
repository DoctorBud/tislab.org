Julie McMurry

Associate Director, TISL

Oregon Health & Science University

Overview

As Associate Director of monarchinitiative.org, I manage the design, development, release, and publication of Monarch Software and integrative data stack. I am cross-trained in biomedical research, public health, project management, software engineering, bioinformatics, and data visualization. My view of research informatics has been shaped by my diverse experiences across the translational divide: academia, the biotechnology industry, hospitals, and the non-profit sector. These experiences have helped me understand the realities of research life and the issues people bring to data collection, annotation, integration, and citation. It has also given me an appreciation for the kinds of social engineering necessary to get technical solutions to succeed.
